About Fast Rent

Our founder

My name is Stefan Topalović, and I am the proud owner of Fast Rent, a successful rent a car company.


I was born and raised in Belgrade, where I completed my elementary education before moving to Obrenovac for high school.


After graduating, I decided to start my own business, and Fast Rent was born.


A few years later, I enrolled at the College of Vocational Studies for Information and Communication Technologies (ICT) to expand my knowledge and stay ahead of the curve in this fast-paced industry.

Fast Rent is a renowned car rental company that provides an excellent service to customers. With years of experience in the industry, the company has gained a reputation for offering reliable, efficient, and affordable car rental services.


The company has a vast fleet of vehicles that includes compact cars, sedans, SUVs, premium and luxury vehicles, among others. We cater to the needs of individuals, families, and businesses, ensuring that every customer finds the right vehicle for their needs.


At Fast Rent, we are committed to providing a hassle-free rental experience, with a focus on customer satisfaction. We understand that renting a car can be stressful, which is why we go the extra mile to make sure our customers have everything they need to make their rental experience smooth and enjoyable.

Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to providing exceptional customer service and support. We are always available to answer any questions or concerns you may have, and we are happy to offer personalized recommendations based on your unique needs.


Whether you need a car for a short-term rental or a long-term lease, Fast Rent is the ideal choice. We take pride in our commitment to excellence, and we look forward to serving you.
